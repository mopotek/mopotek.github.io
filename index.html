Strategic design

I design with outcomes in mind. You'll never see me work on a project without getting some baseline values on what we're trying to fix. Whether it’s reducing time-to-value, improving retention, or validating new opportunities, I use design as a tool to move both business and user metrics. I’m comfortable in ambiguous problem spaces, and I partner closely with product and engineering to define what success looks like. Then I run quick experiments, measure, and iterate our way there.

DesignOps & Leadership

Helping others and being "the connector" brings me tons of fulfilment. I’ve mentored 20+ creatives, coached junior and senior designers alike, and created spaces for honest feedback and growth. Outside of work, I also founded a grassroots tech community to help people connect and learn and ran more than 50 events in my lifetime.

Accessibilty

It's so important to me to ensure my work is accessible to people with disabilities. I strive to create designs that meet WCAG 2.2 AA accessibility standards and enhance user delight across responsive platforms. I shift accessibility left, also training other designers and developers to embed it into every stage of the process.

Playful design

I believe play is a powerful tool for engagement, learning, and emotional connection. At the Social Emotional Technology Lab at UCSC, I co-developed playful research methods tailored for remote settings. My work has unconvered play potentials in unexpected domains, from food tech to drone interactions.

I’ve designed over 13 video games for children, and I aim to bring moments of delight and curiosity into everyday products, even when their purpose is purely functional.

Design systems

Good design at scale needs more than talent, it needs systems to empower decisions to stand the test of time and human error. I build the tools and rituals for teams that make quality design work repeatable: from Figma libraries, design tokens, guiding principles, and Storybook documentation to design critiques. I care about removing friction for my colleagues, clarifying standards, and creating the operational backbone for great design to happen more consistently.

Research & analytics

I’ve ran many discovery research studies, led more than 170 user interviews in various industries, built research operations from the ground up: for example, reaching 66% increase in user-centered and analytics-informed decision making in a company of 60 people as a result of my UX Research democratization and advocacy efforts at Everyday Speech. My approach blends qual and quant, and I focus as much on the systems (recruitment, templates, tagging, repositories) and training people to become researchers, as I do on the insights themselves.
