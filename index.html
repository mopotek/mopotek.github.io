Here is what I love doing!

person_search User and market research

Before starting to design, I must dig deep to understand what the user's problem really is and where it stems from. To do so, I will carry out interviews, questionnaires, focus groups, social media explorations, or build my empathy and understanding of the user's environment through cultural probes.

All steps will be documented with deliverables such as thematic analyses reports, personae, or customer journey maps.

format_shapes Prototyping and design

Once I understood the user's needs and desires, I can move on to the prototyping phase where I will iteratively create wireframes and dynamic prototypes of the final product.

Since the beggining, I will practice participatory design to involve users. For each drafted version, small-scale or big-scale user tests will take place, depending on the projects budget, to make sure the final product is pleasant, usable, and relevant.

phonelink_setup Usability evaluations

I will evaluate how easy (or hard) it is to use the product by carrying out user tests followed by interviews and usability questionnaires.

Bastien and Scapin's or Norman and Nielsen's usability heuristics will be amongst my main evaluation criteriums.

Also expect me to take ergonomy and accessibility for people with diffent kinds of disabilities into account!

engineering Project / Product management

After understanding the needs of the client and the user, I create technical and functional specifications and users stories of what needs to be implemented. This allows me to create a planning, evaluate risks, and delegate tasks to all members of the team.

On a day to day basis, I will follow the advancement of the project and the sprint, communicate internally and externally, and facilitate workshops.
