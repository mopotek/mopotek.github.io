Pillar 2: Level up usability and reliability

Pillar 3: Create clear paths to contribute

Takeaways

  • Co-creation drives adoption: Involving teams early and addressing their pain points ensures buy-in and sustainability
  • Small, iterative improvements matter: Clear rituals and incremental roadmap integration prevent overwhelm.
  • Documentation should reflect the practical experience of building using the design system
  • Metrics guide focus: Clarity surveys, adoption rates, and usage tracking highlight the most impactful actions.
