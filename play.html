    Operation Pizza

    ‍

    I led the Storytelling, UX Design, Interaction Design, and Sound Design of this educational social skills game.

    Operation Pizza is a thrilling competitive video game where two restaurants compete to deliver as many pizzas as possible to win the secret tomato sauce recipe of the world-famous chef Grandpa Pomodoro.

    ‍

    I designed it to for large groups of students by encouraging turn-taking and a multi-player aspect.
